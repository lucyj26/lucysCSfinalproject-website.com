NewYorkBeaches.html New York Beaches

In New York, there are many pretty beaches, one of the highest rated ones is called Rockaway Beach, which is in Queens. It is New Yorks only legal surfing destination and has a huge boardwalk.

The second prettiest beach in New York is called Robert Moses State Park, located in Long Island. It has a 5 mile long coastline with many activities like golfing and volleyball courts.
